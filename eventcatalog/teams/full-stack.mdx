## Overview

The Full Stack team is responsible for developing and maintaining the full stack of the application, including back-end, devops. The team is responsible for building and maintaining the application's architecture, infrastructure, and deployment pipelines. The team works closely with other teams to ensure that the application is scalable, reliable, and secure.

## Responsibilities

- Developing and maintaining the full stack of the application
- Building and maintaining the application's architecture, infrastructure, and deployment pipelines
- Ensuring that the application is scalable, reliable, and secure
- Collaborating with other teams to deliver features and improvements
- Supporting the application in production and troubleshooting issues
- Implementing best practices for software development, testing, and deployment
